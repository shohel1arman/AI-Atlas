Tokens & IDs

0 tokens

Models don't see words — they see token IDs, integers indexing a vocabulary. Common words are one token; rare words split into pieces. Each ID maps to a learned embedding vector.

Next-token probability

The model outputs a probability over the whole vocabulary. Temperature sharpens (low) or flattens (high) the distribution — low is focused and repetitive, high is creative and risky.
