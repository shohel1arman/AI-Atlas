Forward vs. reverse

Forward (training): repeatedly add Gaussian noise to real images until they're indistinguishable from static. The model learns to predict the noise that was added at each step.

Reverse (sampling): start from pure noise and subtract the predicted noise, step by step, steered by your prompt — exactly what the timeline above shows.
